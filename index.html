About

Andrew Mason

I started dabbling in writing after the birth of my two beautiful daughters.

For something to try, I thought I would be able to lift the spirits of children and parents alike by bringing short, uplifting, and positive stories.

At this time I am writing a series of short stories following friends from a small but exsiting town called Sunnyville. In the future I will venture out and work on writing some non-fictional stories for the young adults to enjoy.

I hope you all enjoy these books. If you do, keep your eyes out for the following books in the series
