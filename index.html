About The Author

I started dabbling in writing after the birth of my two beautiful daughters. For something to try, I thought I would be able to lift the spirits of children and parents alike by bringing short, uplifting, and positive stories.

My first book, "The Endless Adventures of Lily in Sunnyville," is the start of a future series of short stories that follow a group of friends from a small town called Sunnyville.

I hope you all enjoy these books. If you do, keep your eyes out for the following books in the series
